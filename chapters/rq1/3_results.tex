\section{Results}
\label{ch:results_rq1}
In this chapter we will present the results of our first experiment.
We will divide the results in two sections. 
The first section will display the results of clustering with all characteristics.
The second section will display the results of clustering without the Levensthein distance.
\newline
During the the process of extracting the characteristics we found that calculating the Levenshtein distance took a long time.
Calculating the Levenshtein distance for the four biggest projects in the data set cost days.
Even for the smaller projects it increased extraction of the characteristics by hours.
Including the Levenshtein distance defeats the purpose of increasing the performance as every bit of performance we increase will be replaced by the cost of calculating the Levenshtein distance.
For this reason we decided to repeat the experiment without the Levensthein distance characteristic.
\newline
Due to the limitation of resources and time we had to omit four source projects from these results. 
The memory needed to store the amount of generated mutants for these projects was too much for the available hardware.
Trying to cluster these mutants this resulted in a out of memory error.
\newline
The results displayed are the average of the results of the 30 repetitions of the experiment.
The result of each single repetition can be found in our Github repository\cite{rbasarat-repo}.
